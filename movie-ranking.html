Movie Ratings - Cult Classics

1. The Big Lebowski (1998)

A quirky tale of mistaken identity, this Coen Brothers' comedy is beloved for its eccentric characters, sharp dialogue, and the unforgettable "Dude." It's a laid-back yet chaotic journey through the absurdity of life.

Donnie Darko (2001)

A mind-bending exploration of time-travel, teenage angst, and existential dread, this film has captivated audiences with its enigmatic plot and haunting atmosphere. It's a dark experience that lingers long after the credits roll.

Rocky Horror Picture Show (1975)

A bizarre, musical celebration of camp and sexual liberation, this movie has become a midnight screening staple. With its catchy tunes and over-the-top performances, it's a flamboyant ode to self-expression and nonconformity.
